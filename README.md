## 🏢 About NCFSS

NCFSS provides premier Facilities, Maintenance, Cleaning, Care, and Support Services. Our mission is to offer top-tier solutions tailored to your property and personnel needs to keep your business running seamlessly.

### Our Core Services

*   **Facilities & Maintenance** - Complete facility upkeep, minor repairs, and proactive maintenance to ensure optimum operational environments.
*   **Cleaning & Environmental** - Industry-leading cleaning solutions prioritizing hygiene, aesthetics, and environmental sustainability.
*   **Care & Support Services** - Non-medical care and dedicated support services delivered with compassion, respect, and professionalism.
*   **Staffing Support Workers** - Providing reliable, trained cleaning and support workers tailored to fill your operational gaps effectively.
*   **Property & Site Support** - Round-the-clock site oversight, security assistance, and general property support ensuring peace of mind.
