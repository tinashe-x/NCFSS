About NCFSS

NC Facilities & Support Services Ltd (NCFSS)

At NCFSS, we provide reliable, high-quality support services designed to make everyday life easier for individuals, homes, and businesses. From cleaning and maintenance to transport and logistics, we are committed to delivering practical solutions with professionalism and care.

We pride ourselves on being dependable, efficient, and detail-oriented, ensuring every job is completed to the highest standard.

Our Values

  • Reliability – We deliver on our promises, on time and without compromise.
  • Professionalism – We uphold the highest standards in every service.
  • Respect – We treat every client and property with care.
  • Quality – We are committed to getting the job done right the first time.
  • Integrity – We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Resourcefulness – We collaborate with trusted partners for excellence.
